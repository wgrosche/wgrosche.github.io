## Profile

German-British Physics graduate (MSc, ETH Zürich) with 3 years of research engineering experience at EPFL CVLab, focused on bridging the gap between control theory, computer vision, and machine learning. Expert in developing high-performance autonomy stacks for high-speed fixed-wing aircraft (100 m/s), with a core strength in translating complex physical systems into tractable optimisation problems.

Work experience
======
* **September 2022 – September 2025: Research Engineer**
  * EPFL Computer Vision Lab (CVLab), Lausanne
  * Supervisor: Professor Pascal Fua
  * Projects:
    * [**AIrcraft**](/portfolio/AIrcraft/) — Time-optimal MPC and trajectory optimisation for a fixed-wing drone targeting F5B competition conditions (100 m/s, ~50 laps of 300 m in 3 min); built a differentiable aerodynamic surrogate model, integrating CFD data and first-principles physical models. (2.5 years)
    * [**MARMOT**](/portfolio/MARMOT/) — Led development of a multi-camera localisation and calibration suite; automated axis-alignment using RANSAC plane fitting and camera height priors. (1 year)

    * [**MultiCam-GT**](/portfolio/multicam-gt/) — Led development of a web-based multi-view annotation tool used in the construction of the [SCOUT dataset](https://scout.epfl.ch/); automated tracklet merging reduced manual annotation time by ~90%. (8 months)
    * **Physics-based Heart and Catheter Simulation** — Catheter–heart interaction simulation for virtual cardiac catheterisation using Cosserat rod model with SDF-based collision modelling. (5 months)
